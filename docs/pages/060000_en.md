# 06 Experimental Techniques and Methods

Laboratory and field methodologies to observe, measure, and characterize hydrological and hydrogeological systems. Includes experimental approaches for determining physical and chemical properties, conducting field investigations, and performing aquifer tests to quantify subsurface flow and transport parameters.
